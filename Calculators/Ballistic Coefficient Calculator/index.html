Ballistic Coefficient Theory

The ballistic coefficient (BC) is a measure of a projectile's ability to overcome air resistance in flight. It's calculated using the formula:

BC = (Mass × Form Factor) / (Diameter² × Density Factor)

Factors Affecting BC

  • Projectile Mass
  • Projectile Shape
  • Cross-sectional Area
  • Air Density
